# Wholesalers — Soweto, Johannesburg & Lenasia

Research shortlist of cash-and-carry / FMCG wholesalers to source stock from, ordered by
distance from the launch zones in `app/config.js` (Meadowlands, Orlando East/West, Dube,
Diepkloof, Zola). Delivery-run distance directly drives the unit economics in the business
plan, so closer is better, all else equal.

⚠️ **Verify before relying on these** — addresses, hours, and contact details come from web
directories/social pages and can go stale. Confirm by phone or an in-person visit before
treating any of these as a committed supplier relationship.

---

## 🥇 Right on Soweto's doorstep — Devland/Nancefield industrial hub
An industrial cluster that sits inside Soweto itself, hosting several wholesale operators
side by side — ideal for a single delivery-run wholesaler visit. **Top recommendation.**

- **Devland Cash and Carry** — 181 Houthammer Rd, Devland, Soweto, 1832
  · 011 989 8800 · info@devland.co.za
  FMCG wholesaler, general merchandise + food, part of a 20+ branch national chain.
- **Metro Cash & Carry (Devland)** — same Devland hub, grocery-focused.
- **Alliance Cash & Carry (Devland)** — 2 Piston Rd, Soweto.

## 🥈 Crown Mines / Selby — ~10–15 min from Orlando/Meadowlands
- **Makro Crown Mines** — 1 Hanover St (cnr Main Reef Rd), Selby, Johannesburg, 2001
  Big-box bulk retail/wholesale — good backup for large one-off buys.
- **Continental Cash & Carry** — 14 Mineral Crescent, Crown Mines, 2092
  · 011 594 2085 · orders@ccnc.co.za
  Groceries, spices, bulk snacks — open Sundays & public holidays too.

## 🥉 Lenasia — ~15–20 min, several strong options
- **5 Star Cash & Carry** — 22 Tugela Crescent, Ext 10, Lenasia · +27 87 821 6742
  Household + perishables.
- **Frontline Cash and Carry / Hyper Lenasia** — 207 Protea Avenue, Ext 7, Lenasia
  · 067 419 9814 · Wholesaler + hypermarket (3 outlets).
- **DB Cash and Carry** — Lenasia · 011 854 5936 · Sweets, stationery, snacks.
- **Rago Cash & Carry** — Lenasia, snack specialist.

## Further out — Johannesburg CBD / East Rand
Useful for price comparison once the pilot is running; not ideal for day-one delivery
logistics given the extra distance.

- **J&E Cash and Carry** — Pretoria-based but a major regional FMCG supplier (groceries,
  personal care, household).
- **SA Cash and Carry** — confirmed branch at 20 Atlas Rd, Boksburg.
- **Yarona Cash and Carry**, **Discount Foods**, **Mega Cash and Carry** — Johannesburg CBD,
  general groceries.

---

## Recommendation for the pilot
**Devland Cash and Carry** is the standout first choice — inside Soweto itself, an
established 20+ branch FMCG chain (not a small independent), minimizing delivery-run
distance. Treat **Makro / Continental at Crown Mines** as the backup for anything Devland
doesn't stock, and the **Lenasia cluster** as a third option or future price-comparison
target as the pilot scales.
